    UNDERSKY

    Installation & performance · 2026

    Stills

    UNDERSKY is a future-historical immersive installation of Earth’s oceanic flora and fauna long after humanity has departed for the stars. Created using public-domain scientific illustrations brought back to life in reconstructed ecologies, the work stages mediated nature in the face of ecological loss, situated within immersive art’s legacy of scientific spectacle.

    BIRDWORLD

    Participatory installation · 2026

    Stills

    BIRDWORLD is a participatory installation of birds migrating across a digital sky.

    As visitors draw their birds, each bird joins a growing flock. Over time the screen fills with hundreds of unique birds, forming an ecology shaped by the creativity of the audience.

    The project explores themes of collective authorship, playful participation, and the joy of observing a shared world. It draws visitor attention and increases lingerability.
